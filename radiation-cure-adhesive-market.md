<p><p>Radiation cure adhesives are advanced bonding agents that solidify when exposed to radiation. The market primarily consists of two types: UV curable adhesives, which cure when exposed to ultraviolet light, offering fast processing and excellent adhesion properties; and electron beam curable adhesives, which utilize high-energy electron beams for curing, providing deep penetration and high bond strength. Both types are widely used in diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and packaging, owing to their efficiency, environmental benefits, and versatility in applications.</p></p>
