<p><strong>The Automotive Power Distribution System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hardwired Vehicle Power Distribution System</li><li>Configurable Vehicle Power Distribution System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Hardwired Vehicle Power Distribution System** uses fixed wiring and connections to distribute electrical power within a vehicle. It is simple, cost-effective, and reliable but lacks flexibility for modifications. In contrast, the **Configurable Vehicle Power Distribution System** employs modular components like fuses, relays, and programmable units, allowing customization and scalability. It supports advanced features like smart power management and diagnostics, making it ideal for modern vehicles with complex electrical demands, though it is more expensive and complex to implement.</p></p>
