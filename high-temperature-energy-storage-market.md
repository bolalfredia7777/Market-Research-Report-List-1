<p><p>High-temperature energy storage includes various technologies designed to store energy efficiently at elevated temperatures. Sodium-sulfur (NaS) batteries excel in large-scale applications, offering high energy density and long life cycles. Sodium-metal halide (NaMx) batteries provide similar benefits but with enhanced safety and operational flexibility. Thermal energy storage (TES) systems focus on storing heat for later use in power generation or industrial processes, utilizing materials like molten salts or phase change materials to retain excess thermal energy effectively.</p></p>
