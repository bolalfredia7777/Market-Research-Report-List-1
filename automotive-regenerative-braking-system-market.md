<p><p>An Automotive Regenerative Braking System (RBS) is an energy recovery mechanism that captures and stores kinetic energy during braking, which would otherwise be lost as heat. RBS is widely used in hybrid and electric vehicles (EVs) to enhance energy efficiency and extend driving range.</p><p>The Automotive Regenerative Braking System Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of electric and hybrid vehicles, stringent emission regulations, and the growing demand for energy-efficient technologies. The Asia-Pacific region is expected to dominate the market due to the rapid adoption of EVs and supportive government policies.</p></p>
