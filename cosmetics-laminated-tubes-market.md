<p><strong>The Cosmetics Laminated Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetic laminated tubes are widely used for packaging beauty and personal care products. The market is predominantly divided into two types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminates) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminates). ABL tubes feature an aluminum layer that provides excellent light and moisture protection, making them suitable for high-end creams and serums. PBL tubes, composed of various plastic materials, offer flexibility and lightweight options, ideal for a range of cosmetic products. Both types enhance product preservation and user convenience.</p></p>
