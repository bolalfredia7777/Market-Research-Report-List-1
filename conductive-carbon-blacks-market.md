SCF (Special Conductive Carbon Black) caters to advanced requirements in electronics and energy storage. XCF (Extra Conductive Carbon Black) delivers superior conductivity for high-performance applications, primarily in batteries and conductive films, ensuring efficient electrical performance.</p></p>
