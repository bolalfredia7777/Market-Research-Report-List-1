<p><p>The Electric Light Skylight market can be categorized into two primary types based on roof design: pitched roof skylights and flat roof skylights. Pitched roof skylights are designed to fit sloped roofs, effectively channeling rainwater and enhancing aesthetic appeal while providing natural light. In contrast, flat roof skylights are tailored for level surfaces, often featuring a low-profile design that minimizes wind resistance. Both types are increasingly popular for energy efficiency, improving indoor lighting, and enhancing the overall ambiance of spaces.</p></p>
