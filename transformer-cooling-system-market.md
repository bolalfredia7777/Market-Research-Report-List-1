<p><ul><li>Air Cooled</li><li>Water Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transformer Cooling System market is primarily categorized into air-cooled and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled transformers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them efficient and low-maintenance, suitable for smaller installations. In contrast, water-cooled systems employ water to absorb and transfer heat, offering superior cooling capabilities, especially for larger transformers in high-load environments. Each type is selected based on specific operational requirements, maintenance considerations, and the cooling efficiency needed to ensure optimal performance and longevity of the transformer.</p></p>
