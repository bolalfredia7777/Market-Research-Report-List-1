<p><p>The Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) devices market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Direct Neural Interface**: Connects directly to neural tissue, enabling direct communication with the brain.</p><p>2. **Synthetic Telepathy Interface**: Facilitates communication between brains without external devices, often through thought transmission.</p><p>3. **Brain-Machine Interface (BMI)**: Integrates brain signals with external machines, allowing for control of devices like prosthetics.</p><p>4. **Mind-Machine Interface (MMI)**: Focuses on user interaction with machines purely through mental commands, enhancing user experience and operational efficiency.</p></p>
