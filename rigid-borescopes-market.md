<p><p>The rigid borescopes market is categorized into two main types: rigid and semi-rigid borescopes. Rigid borescopes are inflexible, offering precise imaging in straight-line inspections, making them ideal for accessing hard-to-reach areas in applications such as aerospace and manufacturing. Semi-rigid borescopes combine flexibility with rigidity, allowing for versatile maneuverability while maintaining a clear view, making them suitable for complex internal examinations in tighter spaces. Both types enhance inspection capabilities across diverse industries by providing high-quality visuals of internal structures.</p></p>
