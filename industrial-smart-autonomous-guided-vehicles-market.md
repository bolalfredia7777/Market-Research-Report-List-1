<p><p>The Industrial Smart Autonomous Guided Vehicles (AGVs) market comprises various guidance technologies. Laser guidance employs lasers to navigate through a predefined path, offering high precision. Magnetic guidance uses magnetic strips or markers on the floor to direct vehicles. Infrared guidance utilizes infrared technology to detect surroundings and facilitate movement. Optical guidance involves cameras and imaging systems to understand the environment for navigation. Additionally, there are other methods that combine various technologies or offer unique guidance solutions tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
