<p><p>GMO corn market types include Herbicide Tolerant (HT), Insect Tolerant (IT), and Others. HT corn is genetically modified to withstand specific herbicides, allowing farmers to control weeds without harming the crop. IT corn is engineered to produce proteins toxic to certain pests, reducing the need for chemical insecticides. The "Others" category includes traits like drought resistance, improved nutrient content, or combined HT and IT traits. These modifications aim to enhance yield, reduce input costs, and improve crop resilience, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
