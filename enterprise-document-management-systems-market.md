<p><p>Enterprise Document Management Systems (EDMS) come in two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems are installed on local servers and provide businesses complete control over their data, ensuring security and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted on external servers, offering flexibility, lower upfront costs, and easier access from various devices. This model typically includes subscription pricing and automatic updates, catering to organizations seeking scalability and reduced IT overhead.</p></p>
