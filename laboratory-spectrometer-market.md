<p><p>The laboratory spectrometer market encompasses three primary types: mass spectrometry, atomic spectrometry, and molecular spectrometry. Mass spectrometry analyzes the mass-to-charge ratio of ions, enabling precise molecular characterization. Atomic spectrometry examines elemental composition through techniques like atomic absorption and emission, crucial for analyzing trace elements. Molecular spectrometry focuses on molecular structure and interactions using methods such as infrared and UV-Vis spectroscopy. Each type plays a vital role in various fields, including pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and materials science, driving innovation and research.</p></p>
