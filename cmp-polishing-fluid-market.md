<p><ul><li>Alumina Slurry</li><li>Colloidal Silica Slurry</li><li>Ceria Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP polishing fluids are essential in semiconductor and optical industries for planarizing surfaces. Alumina slurry, made from aluminum oxide particles, offers effective polishing and is cost-efficient for various applications. Colloidal silica slurry utilizes fine silica particles, providing superior surface finish, making it ideal for advanced semiconductor devices. Ceria slurries, containing cerium oxide, excel in achieving high removal rates and excellent surface quality, particularly for metals and dielectric films. Each type serves distinct purposes in the chemical mechanical planarization process.</p></p>
