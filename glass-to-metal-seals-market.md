<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass-to-metal seals market includes two primary types: matched seals and compression seals. Matched seals consist of precisely engineered glass and metal components designed to fit snugly together, ensuring low thermal expansion and minimal leakage. These are often used in high-performance applications. Compression seals, on the other hand, rely on compressive force to create a tight bond between the glass and metal, accommodating slight variations in dimensions. Both types are crucial in electronic, aerospace, and medical industries for maintaining integrity in demanding environments.</p></p>
