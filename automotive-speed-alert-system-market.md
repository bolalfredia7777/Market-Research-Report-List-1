<p><p>The Automotive Speed Alert System market includes various sensor types that monitor vehicle speed. Optical Vehicle Speed Sensors use light-based technologies to detect wheel rotations, providing precise speed measurements. Hall-type Vehicle Speed Sensors utilize magnetic fields to generate speed readings as a wheel passes through the sensor. Magnetic-electric Vehicle Speed Sensors combine magnetism and electric signals, offering reliable performance in varying conditions. Each type serves to enhance safety and improve driving experience by alerting drivers to speed variations.</p></p>
