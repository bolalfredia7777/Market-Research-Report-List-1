<p><strong>The Micro-Lens Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aspherical Microlens Array</li><li>Spherical Microlens Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-lens arrays market is primarily divided into two types: aspherical and spherical micro-lens arrays. Aspherical micro-lens arrays are designed with a non-spherical shape, allowing for improved light focusing and reduced optical aberrations, making them ideal for applications in advanced optical systems, telecommunications, and imaging. In contrast, spherical micro-lens arrays consist of symmetrical, spherical shapes that are simpler to manufacture and widely used in sensors, lighting, and display technologies, offering effective light collimation and enhancement.</p></p>
