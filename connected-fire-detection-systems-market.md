<p><strong>The Connected Fire Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addressable Connected Fire Detection Systems</li><li>Conventional Connected Fire Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected Fire Detection Systems are classified into two primary types: Addressable and Conventional. </p><p>Addressable Connected Fire Detection Systems identify the specific location of each device in real-time, allowing for precise monitoring and faster response during emergencies. These systems offer enhanced flexibility and customization.</p><p>Conventional Connected Fire Detection Systems, on the other hand, group detectors by zones, triggering alarms for entire areas rather than individual devices. They are generally simpler and more cost-effective but may lack the detailed information provided by addressable systems.</p></p>
