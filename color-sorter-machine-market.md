<p><p>The color sorter machine market is primarily divided into two types: chute-type and belt-type color sorters. Chute-type machines utilize a vertical channel to separate products based on color, delivering high efficiency and accuracy, making them ideal for bulk materials. In contrast, belt-type color sorters use a conveyor belt system to facilitate sorting, offering versatility for a variety of products and suitable for smaller batches. Both types enhance product quality and operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
