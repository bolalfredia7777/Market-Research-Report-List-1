<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Atomic Spectrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atomic Absorption Spectrometry</li><li>X-ray Diffraction Spectrometry</li><li>X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Atomic Spectrometer market is categorized into three primary types: </p><p>1. Atomic Absorption Spectrometry (AAS) measures the concentration of elements by analyzing the absorption of light, primarily used in laboratories for metal analysis.</p><p>  </p><p>2. X-ray Diffraction Spectrometry (XRD) determines the crystalline structure of materials by measuring the angles and intensities of scattered X-rays.</p><p>3. X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometry (XRF) analyzes the elemental composition of materials by detecting the fluorescent X-rays emitted from a sample when excited by an X-ray source. </p><p>These techniques are essential in material characterization and quality control across various industries.</p></p>
