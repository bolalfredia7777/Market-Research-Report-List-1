<p><p>The tin containers market is categorized into two primary types: built-up containers and shallow drawn containers. Built-up containers are assembled from separate components, offering versatility in design and size, making them suitable for a variety of applications. In contrast, shallow drawn containers are formed from a single piece of metal, providing a seamless finish and enhanced durability. Both types cater to consumer needs in packaging, storage, and transportation, with distinct advantages in aesthetic appeal, functionality, and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
