<p><p>The melting furnaces market comprises various types, including cupola, induction, and open hearth furnaces, each serving distinct applications. Cupola furnaces are vertical shaft furnaces typically used for melting cast iron, known for their high efficiency and throughput. Induction furnaces utilize electromagnetic induction for rapid and precise melting, ideal for various metals and alloys. Open hearth furnaces, though less common today, are used for steel production, allowing for large-scale melting and refining. Each type adapts to specific industry needs and material requirements.</p></p>
