<p><p>The diphtheria vaccines market includes several types: DTaP, Tdap, Td, and DT vaccines. DTaP vaccines protect against diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis for young children. Tdap vaccines offer a booster for adolescents and adults, covering the same diseases with a reduced pertussis component. Td vaccines provide protection against tetanus and diphtheria in older populations, particularly as a booster every ten years. DT vaccines are similar to DTaP but do not include the pertussis component, primarily used in specific pediatric cases.</p></p>
