<p><p>The Industrial Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) market is primarily segmented into two types: Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) and Rigid Intermediate Bulk Containers. FIBCs, often made from woven plastic, are lightweight, flexible, and easy to transport, making them ideal for bulk storage and transport of dry goods like chemicals and grains. In contrast, Rigid IBCs are typically constructed from plastic or metal, offering enhanced durability and structural integrity for transporting liquids and hazardous materials, thus catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
