<p><p>The steel refining agent market comprises three primary types: hybrid, pre-melted, and sintered. Hybrid agents combine various refining materials to enhance steel quality and efficiency. Pre-melted agents are produced through an initial melting process, ensuring better consistency and performance in refining operations. Sintered agents, created by compacting and heating raw materials, offer a porous structure that improves chemical reactions during steel refining. Each type serves distinct purposes, influencing the properties and characteristics of the final steel product.</p></p>
