<p><p>The High Performance Eccentric Butterfly Valves market primarily features two types: Double Offset and Triple Offset Butterfly Valves. Double Offset Butterfly Valves incorporate two eccentricities, allowing for better sealing and reduced wear, making them suitable for various flow control applications. In contrast, Triple Offset Butterfly Valves add a third axis, enhancing sealing performance and enabling higher pressure applications. Both types are favored in industries requiring reliable, efficient flow management, such as oil and gas, water treatment, and power generation.</p></p>
