<p><p>The Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) films market comprises three main types: Polycaprolactone TPU, Polyester TPU, and Polyether TPU. Polycaprolactone TPU offers excellent flexibility and is resistant to degradation, making it suitable for applications in medical devices and adhesives. Polyester TPU is known for its superior abrasion resistance and durability, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Polyether TPU provides enhanced hydrolytic stability and is typically used in coatings, textiles, and footwear, balancing performance and versatility across various industries.</p></p>
