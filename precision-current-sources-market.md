<p><ul><li>Unipolar Current Sources</li><li>Bipolar Current Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precision current sources market is divided into unipolar and bipolar current sources. Unipolar current sources provide a constant current in one direction, ideal for applications requiring straightforward control, such as LED driving. In contrast, bipolar current sources can deliver current in both directions, making them suitable for more complex applications like testing or manipulating electronic components. Both types are crucial in industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and medical devices, where accurate and reliable current supply is essential.</p></p>
