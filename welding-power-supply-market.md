<p><strong>The Welding Power Supply Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Current</li><li>Alternating Current</li><li>Pulsed Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding power supply market is categorized into three main types: Direct Current (DC), Alternating Current (AC), and Pulsed Current. DC provides a steady flow of electricity, ideal for most welding applications, ensuring deep penetration and stable arcs. AC alternates the flow, suitable for welding certain materials, particularly aluminum, by providing better cleaning action. Pulsed current combines both, allowing for control over heat input and improving weld quality by reducing distortion and providing versatility in various applications.</p></p>
