<p><strong>The Air Curtains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Re-circulating Air Curtains</li><li>Non-re-circulating Air Curtains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air curtains market is divided into two main types: re-circulating and non-re-circulating air curtains. Re-circulating air curtains draw air from the interior space, creating an effective barrier while helping to maintain indoor temperature and reduce energy consumption. In contrast, non-re-circulating air curtains pull air from the outside environment, primarily used in areas requiring a strong separation between indoor and outdoor spaces, such as entrances of buildings, while minimizing air exchange and contaminants.</p></p>
