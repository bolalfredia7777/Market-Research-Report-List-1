<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes various flaw detection methods to ensure the integrity and safety of aircraft components. Ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves to identify internal defects. Eddy current testing uses electromagnetic induction to detect surface flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic particle testing is effective for identifying surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Laser-based testing offers high precision for measuring surface irregularities. Other methods may include dye penetrant and acoustic emission testing, contributing to comprehensive safety measures in aerospace applications.</p></p>
