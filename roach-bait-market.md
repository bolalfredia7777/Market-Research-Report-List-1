<p><p>The roach bait market comprises three primary types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are enclosed devices that attract and eliminate roaches while preventing access to non-target species. Granular baits are powdery substances that can be scattered in infested areas, appealing to roaches. Gel baits are viscous formulations applied in targeted spots, allowing for precise placement and prolonged effectiveness. Each type offers unique application methods and benefits, catering to various pest control needs and user preferences.</p></p>
