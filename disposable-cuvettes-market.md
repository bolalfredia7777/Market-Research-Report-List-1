<p><p>The disposable cuvettes market primarily features two types: Polystyrene (PS) cuvettes and PMMA cuvettes. PS cuvettes are favored for their cost-effectiveness and clarity, making them ideal for various routine applications. They are lightweight and provide excellent light transmission. In contrast, PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate) cuvettes are valued for their higher thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for more demanding experiments. Both types offer convenient, single-use options that reduce contamination risks in laboratory settings.</p></p>
