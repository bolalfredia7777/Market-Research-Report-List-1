<p><strong>The Micro-RNA Sequencing and Assay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sequencing by Synthesis</li><li>Sequencing by Nanopore</li><li>Ion Semiconductor Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro-RNA sequencing and assay markets comprise various sequencing technologies, each with distinct mechanisms. Sequencing by Synthesis involves the incorporation of labeled nucleotides during DNA synthesis, generating high-throughput data. Sequencing by Nanopore utilizes pH and ionic current changes as single DNA molecules pass through nanopores, allowing real-time sequencing and longer reads. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing measures the release of protons during nucleotide addition, enabling rapid sequencing. Together, these methods facilitate comprehensive micro-RNA profiling for research and clinical applications.</p></p>
