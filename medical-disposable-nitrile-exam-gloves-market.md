<p><p>The medical disposable nitrile exam gloves market is divided into two primary types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a starch-based powder that facilitates easy donning and removal, but may pose allergy risks and contamination issues. In contrast, non-powdered gloves have no additives, reducing potential allergic reactions and increasing tactile sensitivity, making them preferable for many medical professionals. The choice between these types often depends on specific clinical needs and regulatory considerations.</p></p>
