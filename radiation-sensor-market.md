<p><ul><li>CSP</li><li>CerPin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation sensor market consists of various types, including Cesium-137 (CSP) sensors and Ceramic Piezoelectric (CerPin) sensors. CSP sensors are primarily used for detecting and measuring gamma radiation, offering high sensitivity and reliability in various applications, including environmental monitoring and nuclear safety. CerPin sensors, on the other hand, utilize piezoelectric materials to convert radiative energy into electrical signals, facilitating the detection of different radiation types. Both types play crucial roles in security, industrial, and medical applications, ensuring safety and compliance.</p></p>
