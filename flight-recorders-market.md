<p><strong>The Flight Recorders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flight Data Recorder（FDR)</li><li>Cockpit Voice Recorder(CVR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flight recorders market encompasses two key types: Flight Data Recorders (FDRs) and Cockpit Voice Recorders (CVRs). FDRs are devices that capture various flight parameters, such as altitude, speed, and heading, providing crucial data for accident investigation and flight safety analysis. In contrast, CVRs record audio from the cockpit, including pilot conversations and environmental sounds, essential for understanding pilot decision-making during flights. Together, these technologies enhance aviation safety and support investigations into incidents and accidents.</p></p>
