<p><ul><li>Aluminum Oxide Material</li><li>Zirconia Alumina Material</li><li>Silicon Carbide Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cut-off wheel market is categorized mainly into three types based on the abrasive materials used: aluminum oxide, zirconia alumina, and silicon carbide. Aluminum oxide cut-off wheels are versatile and cost-effective, ideal for ferrous metals. Zirconia alumina wheels are designed for high-performance applications, providing durability and resistance to heat, making them suitable for stainless steel and other tough materials. Silicon carbide wheels, on the other hand, are best for cutting non-ferrous metals and brittle materials due to their sharpness and friability.</p></p>
