<p><strong>The Solar Photovoltaics Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocrystalline wafer</li><li>Multicrystalline wafer/Polycrystalline wafer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar photovoltaics wafer market is segmented into two primary types: monocrystalline wafers and multicrystalline (or polycrystalline) wafers. Monocrystalline wafers, made from single-crystal silicon, are known for higher efficiency and space efficiency, making them suitable for residential and commercial applications with limited space. In contrast, multicrystalline wafers, formed from multiple silicon crystals, are generally less expensive to produce and have lower efficiency, making them a popular choice for large-scale solar installations. Both types cater to diverse energy needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
