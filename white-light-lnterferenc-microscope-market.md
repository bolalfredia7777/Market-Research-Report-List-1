<p><p>The White Light Interference Microscope market encompasses various technologies, including Diffraction Grating Interferometers, which utilize gratings to enhance resolution and provide detailed surface profiling. Vertical Scanning or Coherence Probe Interferometers employ vertical scanning techniques to achieve high-precision measurements of surface topography. White Light Scatter-plate Interferometers utilize scatter plates to analyze surface characteristics through light scattering, benefiting applications in material science and biomedicine. Together, these types enhance imaging capabilities and measurement precision across various industries.</p></p>
