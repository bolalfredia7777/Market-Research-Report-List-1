<p><p>The Lithium-ion battery pack market is primarily divided into series and parallel configurations. A series battery pack increases voltage by connecting cells end-to-end, making it suitable for applications requiring higher energy output, like electric vehicles. Conversely, a parallel battery pack links cells side-by-side, enhancing capacity and ensuring a longer runtime without raising voltage, ideal for portable electronics. Each configuration serves distinct needs, impacting efficiency, performance, and application suitability in the growing energy storage sector.</p></p>
