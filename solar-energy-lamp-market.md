<p><p>The solar energy lamp market is divided into two main types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar lamps are self-sufficient, powered by integrated solar panels and batteries, ideal for off-grid areas and providing illumination without reliance on external electricity sources. In contrast, grid-connected solar lamps integrate with the main power grid, allowing excess energy to be fed back, promoting efficiency and sustainability. Both types cater to different consumer needs, enhancing accessibility and energy efficiency in various environments.</p></p>
