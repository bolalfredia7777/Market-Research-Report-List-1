<p><p>Dental Guided Bone Regeneration (GTR) Membranes are specialized barriers used in dental surgery to facilitate the regeneration of bone and tissues around dental implants. <p><p>The Dental Guided Bone Regeneration (GTR) Membrane market encompasses various types designed to facilitate bone regeneration in dental procedures. Collagen membranes promote healing, metal membranes offer durability and structural support, and synthetic membranes provide customizable properties. Additionally, periosteal allografts utilize living tissue to enhance bone regeneration, ensuring effective and stable results in dental implant surgeries.</p></p>
