<p><ul><li>Electric Floor Heaters</li><li>Hydronic Floor Heaters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor heaters market comprises three primary types: electric floor heaters, hydronic floor heaters, and others. Electric floor heaters utilize heating cables or mats to provide warmth directly through the flooring, offering quick installation and efficient operation. Hydronic floor heaters circulate warm water through pipes beneath the floor, delivering even heating and enhanced energy efficiency, ideal for larger spaces. The "others" category may include alternative heating solutions like infrared or solar-powered systems, catering to diverse customer preferences and needs.</p></p>
