<p><p>The medical surgical face masks market is primarily divided into two types: tie-on masks and earloop masks. Tie-on masks feature long straps that are secured behind the head, allowing for a customizable fit, making them suitable for prolonged use in surgical settings. On the other hand, earloop masks are designed with elastic bands that loop around the ears, offering ease of use and quick adjustment. Both types are essential for infection control in healthcare environments, ensuring protection for both healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
