<p><p>MCrAlY coating is a type of metallic alloy coating composed of elements such as Nickel (Ni), Cobalt (Co), Chromium (Cr), Aluminum (Al), and Yttrium (Y). It is widely used in industries like aerospace, power generation, and automotive for its exceptional resistance to high-temperature oxidation, corrosion, and thermal fatigue. The coating is applied through thermal spray techniques, such as high-velocity oxygen fuel (HVOF) and plasma spraying, to protect components like turbine blades, combustors, and other high-stress parts operating in extreme environments.</p><p>The MCrAlY Coating Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period.
