<p><p>The capacitive accelerometer market is categorized into 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis types based on the number of measurement dimensions. A 1-axis accelerometer measures acceleration along a single axis, making it suitable for simple applications like tilt sensors. The 2-axis variant captures movement along two axes, providing more comprehensive data for applications such as gaming and automotive systems. The 3-axis accelerometer measures acceleration across all three spatial dimensions, enabling advanced functionalities in smartphones, drones, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
