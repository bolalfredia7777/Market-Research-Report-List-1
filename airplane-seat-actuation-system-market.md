<p><ul><li>Electromechanical</li><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airplane Seat Actuation System market includes three primary types: Electromechanical, Pneumatic, and Hydraulic systems. Electromechanical systems use electric motors and mechanical components for precise, quiet, and efficient seat adjustments. Pneumatic systems rely on compressed air, offering lightweight and simple operation but with less precision. Hydraulic systems utilize fluid pressure for robust and powerful actuation, ideal for heavy-duty applications but are bulkier and require more maintenance. Each type caters to specific aircraft needs, balancing performance, weight, and complexity.</p></p>
