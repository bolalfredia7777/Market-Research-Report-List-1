<p><p>The calcined magnesium oxide market comprises two main types: caustic calcined (light burned) and heavy burned magnesium oxide. Caustic calcined magnesium oxide is produced at lower temperatures, resulting in a fine, highly reactive powder that is often used in applications like refractory materials and water treatment. In contrast, heavy burned magnesium oxide is produced at higher temperatures, yielding a denser and less reactive product, making it suitable for construction, agriculture, and various industrial applications. Both types serve distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
