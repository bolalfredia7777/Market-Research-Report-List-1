<p><p>The liquid carton packaging market is divided into two main types: flexible and rigid liquid packaging. Flexible liquid packaging involves materials like pouches and bags that can adapt to the shape of their contents, offering lightweight, portable, and cost-effective solutions ideal for beverages and liquid foods. Rigid liquid packaging, on the other hand, includes cartons, bottles, and containers made from sturdier materials, providing durability and protection. This type is commonly used for dairy products, juices, and sauces, ensuring product safety and shelf stability.</p></p>
