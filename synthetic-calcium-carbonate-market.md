<p><p>The Synthetic Calcium Carbonate market comprises two primary types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is derived from natural limestone, mechanically ground into fine particles, and widely used in industries like paper, plastics, and paints. PCC, chemically synthesized from calcium oxide, offers higher purity and controlled particle sizes, making it ideal for specialized applications such as pharmaceuticals and food. Both GCC and PCC cater to diverse industrial needs, with GCC being cost-effective and PCC offering superior quality and performance.</p></p>
