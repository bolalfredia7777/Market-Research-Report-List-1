<p><strong>The Lithium-ion Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer Separator</li><li>Bilayer Separator</li><li>Trilayer Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium-ion separator market consists of three main types: monolayer, bilayer, and trilayer separators. Monolayer separators feature a single polymer layer that provides basic functionality and cost-effectiveness. Bilayer separators combine two distinct layers to enhance thermal stability and improve ionic conductivity. Trilayer separators consist of three layers, offering optimal performance by combining the benefits of both monolayer and bilayer designs, resulting in superior safety and efficiency. Each type caters to different applications and performance requirements in lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
