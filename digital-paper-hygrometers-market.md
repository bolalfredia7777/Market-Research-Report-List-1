<p><strong>The Digital Paper Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Digital Paper Hygrometers</li><li>Relative Digital Paper Hygrometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital paper hygrometers are devices used to measure humidity, categorized into two main types: absolute and relative hygrometers. Absolute digital paper hygrometers provide exact moisture content in the air, typically expressed in grams per cubic meter, offering precise data essential for various applications. Relative digital paper hygrometers, on the other hand, measure humidity as a percentage of the maximum moisture capacity at a given temperature. This relative measurement is widely used in industries like HVAC, agriculture, and food storage, ensuring optimal conditions.</p></p>
