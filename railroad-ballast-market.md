<p><p>The railroad ballast market is categorized into several types based on the geological material used. Granite ballast offers durability and stability, making it a popular choice for high-traffic areas. Trap rock is known for its angular particles, providing excellent drainage and support. Quartzite ballast is highly resistant to weathering, ensuring longevity. Dolomite, characterized by its strength and density, is ideal for maintaining track alignment. Other materials may include limestone and recycled options, catering to diverse engineering needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
