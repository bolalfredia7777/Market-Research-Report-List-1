<p><p>A potentiometer torque sensor is a device used to measure torque by converting mechanical rotation into an electrical signal. It operates on the principle of a potentiometer, where the rotational movement of a shaft alters the resistance, producing a voltage output proportional to the applied torque. These sensors are widely used in automotive, industrial automation, robotics, and aerospace applications due to their simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and reliability.</p><p>The Potentiometer Torque Sensor Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for precise torque measurement in various industries.
