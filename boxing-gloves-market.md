<p><p>The boxing gloves market is categorized into three main types: bag gloves, sparring gloves, and competition gloves. Bag gloves are designed for heavy bag workouts, offering padding for protection and facilitating power training. Sparring gloves are padded for safety during practice sessions with a partner, balancing protection and mobility. Competition gloves are lighter and minimally padded to enhance speed and control during fights, adhering to strict regulations. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to various aspects of boxing training and competition.</p></p>
