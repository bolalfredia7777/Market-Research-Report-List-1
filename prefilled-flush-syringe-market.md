<p><p>The prefilled flush syringe market is segmented into three main types: saline flush syringes, heparin flush syringes, and others. Saline flush syringes contain sterile saline solutions, primarily used to maintain catheter patency and prevent clot formation. Heparin flush syringes are prefilled with heparin, an anticoagulant used to prevent blood clotting in medical devices. The "others" category includes syringes filled with various medications or solutions for specific therapeutic applications, catering to diverse healthcare needs in hospitals and clinics.</p></p>
