<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle barrier gate market is primarily divided into two types: straight and crank barriers. Straight barriers feature a straightforward horizontal arm that can swing or slide to control vehicle access, offering quick and efficient operation for high-traffic areas. Crank barriers, on the other hand, employ a mechanical lifting mechanism, often suitable for locations with limited space or where additional security is needed. Each type caters to different environments, addressing various safety and access needs for both commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
