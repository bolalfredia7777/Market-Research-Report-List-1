<p><p>The radiation-cured inks and coatings market primarily consists of UV curable and EB curable inks and coatings, along with other specialty products. UV curable inks utilize ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, curing the ink instantly upon exposure. EB curable inks, on the other hand, use electron beams for curing, offering deep penetration and rapid drying. Other products may include hybrid formulations and specialized coatings for specific applications. These technologies are increasingly favored for their quick drying times and environmentally friendly properties.</p></p>
