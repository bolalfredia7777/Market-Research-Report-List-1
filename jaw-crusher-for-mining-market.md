<p><p>The jaw crusher market for mining primarily consists of two types: single-toggle and double-toggle jaw crushers. Single-toggle jaw crushers feature a movable jaw mounted on a single toggle plate, allowing for efficient crushing with a simple design, ideal for primary applications. In contrast, double-toggle jaw crushers have two toggles, enabling a more complex motion that results in a deeper crushing chamber, making them better suited for harder materials and higher throughput rates. Each type caters to specific mining needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
