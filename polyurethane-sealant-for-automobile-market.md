<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane sealants in the automobile market are available in two main types: single component and multi-component. Single component sealants are pre-packaged and ready to use, curing upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require mixing different components before application, allowing for customized properties such as enhanced strength or flexibility. These types cater to various automotive sealing needs, ensuring durability and performance in diverse conditions.</p></p>
