<p><p>The Anti-Submarine Rocket Launching System market features three primary launch types: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. </p><p>Hot Launch involves igniting the rocket while still within the launcher, providing immediate thrust and minimizing time before deployment. </p><p>Cold Launch initiates the rocket outside the launcher, typically using gas pressure to expel it first, enhancing safety, especially in confined spaces. </p><p>Concentric Canister Launch utilizes a cylindrical canister design, allowing for multiple rockets to be stored and launched efficiently, maximizing space and operational flexibility.</p></p>
