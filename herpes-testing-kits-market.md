<p><strong>The Herpes Testing Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody/Antigen Based Kit</li><li>Viral Culture Test Kits</li><li>Nucleic Acid Amplification-based Kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Herpes testing kits can be categorized into three main types. Antibody/antigen-based kits detect immune responses or viral proteins, offering quick results for active or past infections. Viral culture test kits involve growing the virus from a sample, providing definitive identification but requiring longer processing time. Nucleic acid amplification-based kits, such as PCR tests, identify the genetic material of the virus, offering high sensitivity and specificity for diagnosing active infections. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in herpes diagnosis.</p></p>
