<p><p>X-ray line detectors are essential components in various imaging systems, categorized into dual-energy, single-energy, and multi-energy types. Single-energy detectors utilize a single photon energy level, delivering straightforward imaging capabilities. Dual-energy detectors operate at two energy levels, enhancing contrast and tissue differentiation. Multi-energy detectors capture a broader range of energy levels, enabling advanced material characterization and improved image quality. Each type serves distinct applications, from medical diagnostics to industrial inspection, tailoring to specific imaging requirements and enhancing detection precision.</p></p>
