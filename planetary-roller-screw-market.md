<p><p>The planetary roller screw market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Standard planetary roller screws utilize multiple rollers to provide high load capacity and efficiency. Inverted roller screws feature a design where the roller races are inverted, enhancing compactness. Recirculating roller screws allow for continuous roller circulation, improving durability and performance. Bearing ring roller screws integrate bearings into the screw design, ensuring smoother motion and reducing wear. Each type serves distinct operational needs across industries such as automation, aerospace, and robotics.</p></p>
