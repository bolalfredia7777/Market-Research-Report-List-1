<p><p>The orthodontics market comprises several key types, including brackets, anchorage appliances, and archwires. Brackets are small attachments bonded to teeth, guiding tooth movement. Anchorage appliances stabilize teeth during treatment to control movement forces. Archwires connect brackets and transmit forces to align teeth effectively. Together, these components facilitate various orthodontic treatments, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences, ultimately enhancing dental aesthetics and functionality. The market continues to evolve with advancements in materials and technology, improving treatment outcomes.</p></p>
